What is EZY LCL?

Ezy LCL is a digital service that is designed to simplify your LCL buying experience.

For each origin & destination on offer, you will get quick access to multiple schedules with fastest transit times at extremely competitive prices. All options provided on EZY LCL are based on direct LCL services only.

It removes the complexities from the buying process, providing you with only the best LCL options available in the market.

With EZYLCL you can receive multiple quotations, make online bookings, access multiple payment options, track you shipments and much more.

In less than 10 minutes, you will be able to make a well informed decision that best meets your business requirement.

This will then be followed by our world class customer service and operations taking care of every minute detail till your shipment is delivered.

Ezy LCL is Powered by DHL, The Worldwide leader in Logistics

TAdvantages of shipping Direct LCL ?

In a Direct LCL option, your shipments loaded at origin CFS are only removed from the container
once the shipment arrives at the destination CFS. See below table for details.

Direct

Shipments moves direct from origin to destination

Container loaded at origin is de-vanned only at destination

Transit time predictable as they are based on carrier schedules

Reduced handling of the cargo

Indirect

Shipments moves from origin to destination via a transhipment point

Container loaded at origin will be de-vanned at transhipment point

Transit times may be unpredictable due to dwell time at transhipment point

Increased handling of cargo due to transhipment activities
