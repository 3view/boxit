How long does a quotation remain valid?

36 hours/ 3 days

What is the source of the Exchange Rates Used?

Rioters

How frequently are the Exchange rates changed?

Weekly (on a Monday) or Daily ?

Can I cancel a Shipment after Confirming the Booking?

Yes, as long as it is before the Cargo Ready Date

How do I update my DGF Credit Account Details?

You can either request for this to be done during the registration process. Or, send an email to GSC_BOXIT_Credit@dhl.com And ask them to update your Credit Account Number into the system.

How can I track my shipment?

Once you have been notified by DHL that the B/L number has been updated into the system. You can use the Manage Booking function to check the shipment record and click on the tracking link which will redirect you to the DHL.com site to view your shipment progress

How do I delete my account if no longer required?

Send an email to the Admin team requesting to delete your personal account
